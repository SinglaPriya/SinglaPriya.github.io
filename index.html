Ruchir Gupta

Florence, Italy

An industrial designer with a focus on product design, furniture design and installation art, Ruchir’s work is an expression of his fascination with exploring new forms and media. He believes we are products of our past – so we understand backwards and live forward. His vast and varied professional work experience, in a relatively short span of time, reflect this philosophy. He has worked on creating innovative design solutions for urban and rural target audiences, for FMCG as well as localised brands, working with a wide range of materials. His personality – open-minded, receptive to both appreciation and disapproval, and interactive – finds expression in both his work and work ethics.
